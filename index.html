A New Life of True Freedom

One of the deepest desires of the human heart is to truly know God.

Paul expresses this longing in Philippians 3:10. He desired not just knowledge about Christ, but a real encounter with Him.

Jesus speaks of true freedom in John 8:36. Freedom is not doing whatever we want. True freedom is found in Him.

Proverbs 14:12 reminds us that what seems right can lead to destruction.

Romans 3:4 declares that God remains true regardless of human opinion.

Galatians 3:27 reveals that we put on Christ and become new.

Romans 6:1 shows that grace leads to transformation, not continued sin.

2 Corinthians 5:17 declares that we are made new.

Prayer

Lord Jesus, help me know You deeply. Teach me to walk in Your truth and live as a new creation. Amen.
