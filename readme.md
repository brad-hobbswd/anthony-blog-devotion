# Anthony's Devotion & Ministry

A ministry website dedicated to sharing Scripture, devotion, worship, encouragement, and faith centered on Jesus.

## Live Website

🌐 https://brad-hobbswd.github.io/anthony-blog-devotion

## About

Anthony's Devotion & Ministry provides:

- Biblical devotionals
- Scripture-based encouragement
- Worship resources
- Ministry connections
- Faith-building content centered on Jesus

The site is designed to offer uplifting devotionals, meaningful reflection, and worship resources for believers seeking to grow in their walk with God.
